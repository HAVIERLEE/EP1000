Introduction to the Basic Safety Rule

Safety should be everyone's number one prioity. In this section I will going through the Fablab Safety Rules.

1. Wear proper attire while in the Fablab. Things to take note:

  • Wear Long Pants & Covered Shoes
  • No long sleeved shirts, loose attire, loose items
  • Long Hair must be tied up

2. Always report all injuries no matter how minor to the staff in charge

3. Do not operate any machinery unless you are trained and certified in its usage and are given permission to do so.

4. Emergency Procedures:

  • If the alarm sounds, stop work immediatly and wait for staff in charge.
  • If a fire starts in the lab inform everyone immediatly and leave the lab.
  • When reporting emergencies please seek the help of the staff.

5. You must keep the workplace clean, neat and orederly after use:

  • Clean up debris and chips.
  • Returning tools to their designated storage areas
  • Make sure to recycle any leftover materials.

5. Report all unsafe conditions or acts to the staff in charge.

6. Wear Personal Protective Equipment when operating any equipment.

7. Follow machine operating procedures.

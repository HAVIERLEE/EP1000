Project Title: Nerf Dart Launcher

What does it do?

Nerf Dart Launcher that is controlled by a rotary encoder that fires a nerf dart.

What are it's features?

The Rotary encoder controls the X axis and Y axis of the Dart Launcher with the angles being displayed on a LCD. Once the chosen X and Y axis is made, the encoder is pressed for 3 seconds before it is fired

What will I make

Process Features
CAD Design, Graphics I will use CAD to design the base, the mount for the turret and the turret of the launcher.
Laser cutting I will laser cut the base of Launcher.
3D Printing I will 3D print a mount and the turret of the Launcher.
Micro-controller I will be using an Arduino to Control the Servo Motor
Input devices Rotary Encoder
Output devices 3 Servo Motor, X axis, Y axis and Trigger for the Turret. LCD Display meant for displaying the Angle of the Motors.

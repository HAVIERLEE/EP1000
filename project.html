Project Title: Sentry Gun

What does it do?

An automated toy gun that shoots at targets, either automatically or manually targeted using my computer.

What are it's features?

A Webcam detects looks for a target shoot at them using the toy gun attach on it. The Sentry gun has several modes, one fires a warning shot at a target, another just watches over an area, and one just fires at an available target.

What will I make

Process Features
CAD Design, Graphics I will use CAD to design the entire sentry gun, including the base and turret.
Laser cutting I will laser cut the base of the sentry gun.
3D Printing I will 3D print a mount for the Nerf Gun to attach to the base.
Micro-controller I will be using an Arduino Uno to move the stepper motor and a servo motor to control when to trigger the toy gun.
Input devices Web camera
Output devices Stepper Motor and Servo Motor

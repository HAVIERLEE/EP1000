HOW TO DRAW A KNIGHT CHESS PIECE IN FUSION 360

Tip 1: Filleting the edges for a more smooth look

❮ ❯

Step 1: Second on the left of the modify tab, you will see the Fillet function. Click on it.

Step 2: Once you have chosen it, select the edges of the Kngiht pieces. Once you are satifised with how it looks you can hit enter to fillet it. Note that the radius of your fillet might effect how it looks. If it is too large, it may remove chunks of your workpiece.
